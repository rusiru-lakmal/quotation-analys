ELABORATION OF THE BENEFITS PAID
1) BASIC LIFE COVER
A LUMP SUM AMOUNT WILL BE PAYABLE AT THE UNFORTUNATE EVENT OF DEATH DUE TO AN ACCIDENT OR SICKNESS.
2) ACCIDENTAL DEATH BENEFIT
IF THE DEATH OCCURS AS A RESULT OF AN ACCIDENT, THE COMPANY SHALL PAY A LUMP SUM AMOUNT AS INDICATED ABOVE.
3) TOTAL PERMANENT DISABILITY DUE TO ACCIDENT & SICKNESS
AT THE UNFORTUNATE EVENT OF A TOTAL PERMANENT DISABILITY DUE TO ACCIDENT A LUMP SUM PAYMENT OF 60% OF THE
COVER AMOUNT WILL BE PAYABLE AND THE BALANCE SHALL BE PAID OVER A PERIOD OF FOUR YEARS IN EQUAL PROPORTIONS
SUBJECT TO THE INSURED REMAINING TOTALLY AND PERMANENTLY DISABLE DURING THOSE YEARS.
AT THE UNFORTUNATE EVENT OF A TOTAL PERMANENT DISABILITY DUE TO SICKNESS, 20% OF THE COVER AMOUNT WILL BE
PAYABLE EVERY YEAR FOR A PERIOD OF 5 YEARS SUBJECT TO THE INSURED BEING TOTALLY AND PERMANENTLY DISABLE
DURING THOSE YEARS.
4) PARTIAL PERMANENT DISABILITY DUE TO ACCIDENT (PPD)
IN THE EVENT OF DISMEMBERMENT DUE TO AN ACCIDENT, A PROPORTION OF THE ABOVE COVERAGE SHALL BE PAYABLE
